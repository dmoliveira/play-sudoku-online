Strategy guide

Sudoku strategies that actually help

Begin with simple scanning. Then use notes to track candidates in uncertain cells. As puzzles become harder, work in cycles: place one strong value, then re-scan the whole board for fresh openings.

Good habits matter more than speed at first. Speed comes later, once your eye learns to see missing numbers, strong pairs, and narrowed boxes quickly.
